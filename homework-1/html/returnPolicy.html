Return & Refund Policy

Clients can request project cancellations or refunds within 14 days of delivery if the service does not meet agreed terms.

  • Unused Services: Refunds apply only to services that haven’t been accessed or used.
  • Valid Reason: Requests must include a clear reason and may require additional documentation.
  • Partial Refunds: Available in special situations, subject to review and approval.
  • Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
